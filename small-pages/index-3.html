Your Ultimate Guide to Skiing During Covid

Dec 10, 2020

Yes, ski season is on, people--although it's going to look a lot different this year. The good news: Mountains accross the country are coming up with creative ways to keep skiers and snowboarders safe on and off the slopes. And while that may cause a few more planning headaches (book in advance!), we're more than willing to deal if it means we get to shred powder with the sun on our face and wind at our back (followed by a steaming cup of coco slopeside).

If you're like us, you're all in but need the details before developing your action plan. So we've come up with an exhaustive guide to this very, very different ski season. From the new Covid-19 rules and regulations and tips for first-time skiers to the most unlikely places to find a ski bum (Kazakhstan!?) and a comprehensive list of every resort in America, here's what every pow-hungry man or woman should know.
